Flux Planner

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Flux Planner is a student and educator productivity tool by Azfer Mohammed. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it — for students, teachers, counselors, and staff.

1. What We Collect

We collect only what's necessary to make the app work:

  • Account info — name, email, and profile photo when you sign in (Google or guest upgrade)
  • Planner data — tasks, grades, notes, habits, goals, schedule, and study tools you enter
  • Educator data — classes, assignments, submissions, announcements, and messages you create as a teacher or staff member (stored in Supabase with row-level security)
  • Gmail data — email subjects and senders only (read-only, only if you connect Gmail)
  • Google Calendar — upcoming events (read-only, only if you connect Calendar)
  • Google Classroom & Drive — read-only course/file metadata for educator imports when you connect Workspace
  • Canvas LMS — course and assignment data when you authorize your school Canvas login
  • School info — locker, student ID, class schedule, and role profile you optionally enter
  • Device-local data — some staff widgets (e.g. Quick-Grade buckets, workspace layout) stay on your browser unless synced elsewhere by you

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To sync your planner and educator data across your devices
  • To power Flux AI and optional connected models (only what you send or enable)
  • To show Gmail tasks, Calendar events, and Google/Canvas imports inside the app
  • To run optional Claude MCP connections you explicitly authorize
  • We never sell your data or use it for advertising

3. Where Data Is Stored

  • Supabase — planner and educator data in PostgreSQL with row-level security; users can only access their own rows unless school flows you enable allow otherwise
  • Your device — localStorage and sessionStorage cache for offline use and staff widget layout
  • AI providers — Groq (default chat), Google Gemini (vision), and optional models you connect; each provider's policy applies to content you send
  • Claude MCP — OAuth tokens are stored hashed server-side; only allowlisted planner keys are readable/writable via the connector

4. Google API Scopes We Request

  • gmail.readonly — show email subjects/senders as tasks
  • calendar.readonly and calendar.events — read events; write only when you explicitly use calendar features that create events
  • classroom.courses.readonly — educator course imports (read-only)
  • drive.readonly — educator file imports (read-only)
  • We do not send email on your behalf or modify Drive files without your action
  • We do not sell or share Google user data with third parties except AI/import processors you authorize

5. Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time from Settings → Data → Clear All Data. This removes your data from both your device and our servers immediately.

6. Children's Privacy

Flux Planner is designed for high school students (14+). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this app.
