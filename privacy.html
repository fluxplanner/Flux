Flux Planner

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Flux Planner is a student productivity tool by Azfer Mohammed, built for students. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. What We Collect

We collect only what's necessary to make the app work:

  • Google Account info — your name, email, and profile photo when you sign in with Google
  • Planner data — tasks, grades, notes, habits, goals, and schedule you enter into the app
  • Gmail data — we read email subjects and senders only (read-only access, only if you connect Gmail)
  • Google Calendar data — we read upcoming events (read-only access, only if you connect Calendar)
  • School info — locker number, student ID, and class schedule you optionally enter

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To sync your planner data across your devices
  • To power the Flux AI tutor (your context is sent to Groq's API to generate responses)
  • To show your upcoming Gmail tasks and Calendar events inside the app
  • We never sell, share, or use your data for advertising

3. Where Data Is Stored

  • Supabase — your planner data is stored securely in a Supabase PostgreSQL database with row-level security. Only you can access your own data.
  • Your device — data is also cached locally in your browser's localStorage for offline access
  • Groq API — chat text is sent to Groq (groq.com) for processing; Groq's privacy policy applies.
  • Google Gemini — when you attach an image for analysis, that request is sent to Google's Gemini API; Google's terms and privacy policy apply.

4. Google API Scopes We Request

  • gmail.readonly — to show your school emails inside the app as tasks
  • calendar.readonly — to show upcoming events on your planner calendar
  • We do not modify, delete, or send any emails or calendar events
  • We do not share Google user data with third parties except as described above

5. Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time from Settings → Data → Clear All Data. This removes your data from both your device and our servers immediately.

6. Children's Privacy

Flux Planner is designed for high school students (14+). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this app.
